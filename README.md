# BJJ Performance Tracker

Your personal BJJ training, nutrition, and weight tracker.

---

## Deploy to your phone in ~5 minutes

### Step 1 — Upload to GitHub

1. Go to **github.com** and sign up (free)
2. Click **"New repository"** → name it `bjj-tracker` → click **Create**
3. Click **"uploading an existing file"**
4. Drag and drop the entire `bjj-tracker` folder contents:
   - `index.html`
   - `package.json`
   - `vite.config.js`
   - `src/` folder (with `main.jsx` and `App.jsx` inside)
5. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

### Step 2 — Deploy on Vercel

1. Go to **vercel.com** → sign up with your GitHub account (free)
2. Click **"Add New Project"**
3. Select your `bjj-tracker` repository
4. Vercel auto-detects Vite. Just click **"Deploy"**
5. In ~60 seconds you get a live URL like:
   `https://bjj-tracker-abc123.vercel.app`

---

### Step 3 — Add to your phone home screen

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down → tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Chrome
2. Tap the **3-dot menu**
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**

It will appear as an app icon and open full screen, no browser bar.

---

## Backing up your data

Your data is stored in your phone's browser (localStorage).
It's safe as long as you don't clear your browser data.

**To back up:** Tap the ☁ icon in the top-right → "Download backup"
**To restore:** Tap ☁ → "Select backup file" → pick your `.json` file

Back up whenever you want — before clearing browser data, switching phones, etc.

## Features

- **Home** — Daily readiness check-in, quick stats, next workout suggestion, personal records
- **Gym** — Push / Pull / Legs logging with weight progression tracking, session history, edit/delete, monthly calendar, full program editor
- **Food** — 3,300 kcal goal, macro tracking, saved meals, 7-day chart
- **Weight** — Progress to 170 lbs, trend line, ETA calculator, 7-day average
